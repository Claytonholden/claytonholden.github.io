Topic 2 — Filtering & Baseline Analysis

Week 3 focused on learning what “normal” looks like. I started with no filters, then applied display filters to reduce noise, identify dominant hosts and protocols, and build a baseline across multiple captures.

Display Filters Protocol Hierarchy Endpoints Conversations

Key Takeaway

The biggest lesson from this week was that baselining comes before “threat hunting.” If you don’t understand normal behavior, everything looks suspicious. Filters let you isolate the important traffic and justify observations with evidence.
